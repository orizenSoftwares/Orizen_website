Headless eCommerce Implementation

Headless eCommerce solution is the kind of arrangement where frontend (Storefront) & Backend (functionalities) aren’t interdependent. You can choose the frontend of your system and integrate your backend with it. In case of headless eCommerce the frontend and backend are independent of each other and can be modified without impacting each other. This allows developers to choose a tech stack that they want to work in.

Although working on frontend and backend development is a tedious task, for future the updates become quite easier.
