MEDICAL BILLING SOFTWARE

Our medical billing solution is mainly for avoiding error rates and increasing the rate of claims. This allows the user to better control the sales cycle and accounts receivable. With our solution, you can overcome billing issues, maximize accuracy and efficiency.

MEDICAL CLAIMS PROCESSING

Cloud-based healthcare billing solution and medical claim processing software help healthcare organizations of all sizes increase revenue, bring the cost down associated with managing insurance claims and patient payments.

ELECTRONIC PRIOR AUTHORIZATION

Manual prior authorization is time-consuming, expensive and frustrating. integrates directly with electronic health records (EHRs), enabling healthcare professionals to easily obtain prior authorizations. Our ePA delivers approvals in minutes.

COMPLAINTS MANAGEMENT SOFTWARE

Development of billing management system to easily collect billing data, certify and submit to insurance company. We offer integrate iFrame widgets for submitting customer complaints, reserves forecasting and price optimization modules.

MOBILE INSURANCE APPLICATION

We develop hybrid mobile applications for client self-service and submission of complaints. Rule-based access, real-time agent tracing, custom form, photo evidence attachment and catalog creation and payment processing programming through APIs.

DOCUMENT MANAGEMENT SYSTEM

A user-defined document capture module integrated with cloud host and policy form creation advanced real-time for organization and database queries for contracts, policies, approvals, licenses, and marketing materials function and document classification system.
